How this restaurant all began...

On a dark cold winter, a 16 y/o girl named Ruby was lying motionlessly in an abandoned house. Her face was very pale and was on the brink of death. Ruby was abandoned by her father in the streets when she was 6. Ever since then, she had been living homeless. Suddenly, a boy, around her age appeared next to her out of nowhere. His eyes sparkled brightly and beautifully like a blue galaxy. She couldn't hear his footsteps as she was born deaf.

That boy, later named Aqua, opened her mouth and dropped a pill in it. A few seconds later, her limbs gained strength and she rose slowly. When compared to her life, Ruby was more elated when she could hear sounds for the first ever. Aqua invited her to live in his world where shelter and warm food will be provided. Ruby could not agree more. Aqua's world was a land where magic lived.


Two years had passed. Ruby had never felt pure joy like this. Every day, Aqua would cook gourmet dishes for Ruby and even taught her to cook using his magical method. One day, while Ruby and Aqua were harvesting vegetables in an open field, a large beastly creature with sharp fangs appeared behind Ruby. Ruby was immediately taken up the air with force and thrown into its mouth. Suddenly a quick whooshing arrow was sent flying into its eyes. Aqua jumped into its mouth to save Ruby who was stuck in its teeth. Aqua finally told Ruby the truth that if he didn't steal the medicine made from the beasts' eggs, the beast wouldn't hunt her down.


The creature wailed and ran angrily towards a nearby ocean. It plunged deep into the waters together with Ruby and Aqua. Both of them fought the crashing waves futilely to reach the surface. Thinking fast, Aqua created a portal in the water and pushed Ruby into it. Ruby was puzzled about why Aqua didn't follow together. Later, Ruby realised that it would be the last time they'll ever see each other. A split of seconds later, Ruby found herself back in that old abandoned building where she first met Aqua.


Ever since Ruby took that medicine, her eyes turned purple galaxy-looking like Aqua. Tears streamed down her sparkly eyes. She felt like she has been in a very long sweet dream. She got up but felt confused about why there was a sharp pang of sadness in her heart as if she had lost someone or something precious to her.

Ruby continued living nomadically from place to place. One day, she walked past an old lady who struggled in her food business stall. Out of reflex, Ruby walked up to her as if she knew the solution to help her. The owner was surprised as Ruby managed to attract 3 times her usual customers in a day. At first, no one wanted to purchase her food. But, she did not give up easily as she had a strong faith in that secret recipe. And indeed, customers slowly liked her dishes, commenting the food tasted surreal as if mixed with magical substance. The owner trusted Ruby a lot so she gave up her business to Ruby.


Due to its unusually high popularity, Ruby's small stall had grown into a fine dining restaurant just within a year. She named her restaurant yume, meaning dream in Japanese. Up till this day, she dreamed and hoped to meet that special person again.
